Welcome to Yakult Australia

Based in Dandenong, Victoria, Yakult produces the highest quality fermented milk drinks—Yakult Original and Yakult LIGHT—using the Lacticaseibacillus paracasei Shirota (LcS) strain, exclusive to Yakult.

We continue to educate Australians about the benefits of this unique probiotic strain.

What is Yakult?

Yakult is a fermented milk drink containing a high concentration of the Lacticaseibacillus paracasei Shirota strain.
